Fine-Tuning Lightweight Transformer-Based Models for Event Management SMEs

This study evaluates lightweight Transformer models—FLAN-T5, BART, GPT-2, and Qwen 2.5-0.5B—for SME-focused chatbot applications using a Retrieval-Augmented Generation pipeline. A dataset of 57,879 training examples was created from 44 public SME websites and forums. Although FLAN-T5 achieved the highest evaluation scores, Qwen 2.5 was selected for the EaseAI chatbot because of its more natural, customer-oriented responses. The study also recommends quantization for efficient deployment on low-resource systems.
